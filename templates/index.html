SCIENCE RESEARCH COMPETITION AMONG UNDERGRADUATES IN INSTITUTIONS IN LAGOS STATE INTRODUCTORY PAGE

The science and technology research competition themed: “science and technology; a tool to a sustainable economy” is open to undergraduates in science, engineering, and allied fields of higher institutions of learning in Lagos state. The research projects are expected to resolve one or more challenges encountered in Lagos state. The research projects are expected to be maiden, unique, and made up of 100% local content. Applicants are to upload a 1 – 5 minute video presentation on their research project.
